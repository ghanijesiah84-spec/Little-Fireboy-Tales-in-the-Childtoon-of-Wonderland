## 🌟 Franchise Overview

"Little Fireboy Tales in the Childtoon of Wonderland" is a vibrant, imaginative series that captures the essence of early 2000s animation. Set in the whimsical **Childtoon of Wonderland**, the show follows the adventures of **Little Fireboy** and his diverse group of friends as they navigate a world where creativity and wonder are the ultimate powers.

### 📺 The Television Series (Seasons 1 & 2)
The complete series spans **128 episodes** across two action-packed seasons. Each 11-minute episode is a self-contained adventure exploring themes of friendship, problem-solving, and the power of imagination.

*   **[Full Episode Guide](docs/series/episode_list.md)**: Titles and synopses for all 128 episodes.
*   **[Series Bible](docs/series/series_bible.md)**: Core concepts, world-building, and production details.
*   **[Character Biographies](docs/series/character_bios.md)**: Detailed profiles of Fireboy, Watergirl, and the Wonderland gang.

### 🎬 Little Fireboy the Movie
An epic feature-length adventure that expands the world of Wonderland. When a mysterious "Grey Gloom" threatens to drain the color and music from the world, Fireboy and his friends must embark on their greatest quest yet.

*   **[Movie Synopsis](docs/movie/movie_synopsis.md)**: Plot points, themes, and key moments from the film.

### 🎵 Original Soundtracks (OST)
A massive musical library composed by **Steve Bernstein & Julie Bernstein**, featuring a blend of orchestral richness and playful energy.

*   **[TV Series Soundtrack (300 Tracks)](docs/series/series_soundtrack.md)**: The complete 300-track MP3 collection listing.
*   **[Movie Soundtrack (78 Tracks)](docs/movie/movie_soundtrack.md)**: The cinematic 78-track MP3 collection listing.
