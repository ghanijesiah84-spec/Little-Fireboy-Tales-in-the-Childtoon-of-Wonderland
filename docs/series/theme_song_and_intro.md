# Little Fireboy Tales in the Childtoon of Wonderland: Theme Song & Intro

## Opening Theme Song: "Spark of Wonder"

**Genre:** 2000s Pop-Punk / Alt-Rock (Style of *Danny Phantom* or *Kim Possible*)
**Tempo:** Fast, energetic, and driving.

---

**(Intro - Heavy distorted guitar riff, fast drums)**
